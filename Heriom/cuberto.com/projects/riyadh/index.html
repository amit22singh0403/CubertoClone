The challenge

We were entrusted with developing a brand new website for Saudi Arabia’s capital, Riyadh, in collaboration with AXIS, a top-tier local agency. Riyadh is a modern, rapidly expanding metropolis with a population of 6.5 million people. Not many companies in the world can say that they have taken on projects of this size and complexity, but Cuberto sure can.
